Help

How do I report a technical issue with the website?

If you encounter a technical issue with the website or online tools, please report it to the IT by emailing us at help@bobcathub.com. Provide as much detail as possible about the issue, including any error messages you may have received.

What should I do if I forgot my student ID or password?

If you forgot your student ID or password, please contact the school's IT department or the administrative office. They will assist you in recovering or resetting your credentials.

What resources are available for academic support?

The school offers various resources for academic support, including tutoring services, writing centers, and study groups.
